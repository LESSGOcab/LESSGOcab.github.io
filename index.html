Frequently Asked Questions


Can I have a lost item delivered to me?

Yes, if you've lost an item during your ride, you can contact our support team to arrange for its return.

Can I rent a car using LESSGO?

At the moment, we only provide cab services. However, we're continuously exploring options to enhance our services, including car rental.

Can I request a ride that picks up friends in different locations?

Currently, our platform supports single-location pickups. Each ride request should have a single pickup location.

Can I request a taxi on LESSGO?

LESSGO provides cab services operated by our network of drivers. While it's similar to a taxi service, our platform offers additional features and convenience.

Is there a ride option for a group of 10 people?

At the moment, our ride options cater to a maximum of seven passengers. For larger groups, you may need to request multiple rides.
