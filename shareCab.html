Share a Ride, Share the Comfort with LESSGO

What is "Share cab at your comfort"?

It's our innovative solution to a common dilemma: how to enjoy the benefits of a shared ride without compromising on comfort. With this feature, you can choose to share your ride with other passengers who are headed in the same direction – but only when it suits you.

How does it work?

  1. Book with Ease: Start by booking your LESSGO cab as usual.
  2. Opt-In to Share: If you're open to sharing your ride, simply select the 'Share cab' option.
  3. Match Made in Transit: Our smart algorithm finds compatible co-riders who share your route.
  4. Confirm Your Comfort: Before we match you, you'll get to confirm if you're comfortable with the co-rider and the new estimated arrival time.
  5. Enjoy the Journey: Sit back and enjoy the ride, knowing you've saved money and made a new acquaintance.

Why choose to share?

  • Economical: Sharing means savings. Split the fare and keep your wallet happy.
  • Eco-Friendly: Fewer cars on the road translate to lower carbon emissions.
  • Social: Meet new people and make connections on the go.

Your Comfort, Your Terms

We understand that sharing a ride is a personal choice. That's why we've made it completely optional and customizable to your preferences. Whether it's rush hour or a relaxed afternoon, share a ride on your terms.

Join us in redefining city travel. With LESSGO's "Share cab at your comfort," you're not just choosing a smarter way to travel; you're choosing a journey that's as unique as you are.
